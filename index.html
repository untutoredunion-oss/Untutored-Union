FAQ

Common questions — we keep everything clear before starting.

Do you work with individuals (menus, wedding cards, invites)?
Yes. We support individuals for select projects, especially print collateral and personal branding. Share the brief and timeline and we’ll confirm fit.
How do you price projects?
Typically fixed-scope project pricing. For ongoing needs we can offer monthly design support. We confirm scope before quoting.
Do you do CAD/manufacturing work for every project?
No. CAD and technical depth are included only when the project requires it. Many projects are purely branding/packaging/docs/video.
Can you sign an NDA?
Yes. If your project requires confidentiality, we can work under an NDA before you share files.
What files will I receive?
We deliver a clean package: editable source files + exports (AI/PDF/PNG/MP4/etc.). For CAD, we deliver the agreed formats (STEP/SLDPRT/etc.).
